CapyParty Support

Help with nearby play, travel preparation, TestFlight, and Group Play purchases.

Email support@tickitocki.com

Prepare before travelling

  1. Install the same current CapyParty build on every device.
  2. Open CapyParty once and allow Local Network access.
  3. Purchase or restore Group Play while internet access is available, if needed.
  4. Host a test party, connect every family device, launch two games, and reconnect one device.
  5. In airplane mode, turn Wi-Fi back on. Nearby play does not require an internet connection after preparation.

If a nearby party does not appear

  • Confirm Wi-Fi is enabled on every device, including in airplane mode.
  • Open iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Local Network and enable CapyParty.
  • Keep the devices nearby and leave CapyParty open while joining.
  • Confirm all devices use a compatible current build.
  • Leave the room and retry Host or Join before restarting devices.

Invitations and TestFlight

During beta, install with the CapyParty public TestFlight link. After installation, tap the original party invitation again while near the host. Typing the invitation into Safari is not the same as tapping a Universal Link from Messages, Mail, or Notes.

Restore Group Play

Use Restore Purchases in CapyParty while connected to the internet and signed into the Apple Account that owns or receives the Family Sharing purchase. Apple handles billing and refunds; CapyParty cannot modify an App Store transaction.

What to include in a support request

Include the app version and build, device models, iOS/iPadOS versions, host or guest role, number of connected devices, selected game, Local Network permission state, airplane/Wi-Fi state, reproduction steps, and any displayed CP-NET- failure code. If available, attach the privacy-safe connection report exported by the app.

Do not send device private keys, full device fingerprints, payment-card details, precise location, Apple Account credentials, or a child’s name.
